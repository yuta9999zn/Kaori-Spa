Setup Guide

Follow these steps to point your custom domain to our platform.

  1. 1

    Add DNS Record

    Log into your domain provider (e.g., GoDaddy, Namecheap) and add a new CNAME record.

  2. 2

    Configure Record

    Type CNAME
    Host / Name booking
    Value / Target domains.auraplatform.com
  3. 3

    Verify Connection

    Click "Verify Domain" in Section 2. Propagation may take up to 24 hours.
